Solaris - Our Solar System

Venus, the second planet from the Sun and sixth largest in our solar system,
holds the title of the hottest planet. Named after the goddess of love,
Venus is often dubbed Earth's twin due to its similar size and proximity.
However, despite its benign namesake,
Venus presents a hellish environment.
Its surface temperature can melt lead,
and its dense atmosphere reduces the
Sun to a mere blur of light from the surface.
